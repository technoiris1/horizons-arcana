# Horizons Arcana

## What is Arcana?
 This July, 100+ teen hackers from all around the world will gather in Singapore for a 3-day long hackathon Horizons Arcana. Arcana is being organised by Hack Club, Hack Club is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit that helps over 50,000 high school makers all around the world. Food and accomodation will be provided, this hackathon is 100% free for all the attendees. Travel stipends will be provided too! Our goal is to make this event accessible to everyone regardless of their financial or geographical background.

 ### What is Hack Club?
 Hack Club is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit (EIN: 81-2908499) that helps high school students learn to code and build projects. We’re the largest teen-led coding community, with over 50,000 students building projects with their friends in Hack Club each year. You can learn more about Hack Club at hackclub.com!
