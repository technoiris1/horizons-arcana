Overview

What is Arcana?

This July, 100+ teen hackers from all around the world will gather in Singapore for a 3-day long hackathon Horizons Arcana. Arcana is being organised by Hack Club, Hack Club is a 
501(c)(3) nonprofit that helps over 50,000 high school makers all around the world.

This hackathon is 100% free for all the attendees. Travel stipends available!

What is Hack Club?
Hack Club is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit (EIN: 81-2908499) that helps high school students learn to code and build projects. We’re the largest teen-led coding community, with over 50,000 students building projects with their friends in Hack Club each year. You can learn more about Hack Club at hackclub.com!

Some of the past events we've run are-
1. Overglade- A 4-day long Game Jam in Singapore.
2. Juice- A 2-month long Game Jam followed by a week-long popup cafe in Shanghai!
3. Undercity- A 4-day long Hardware Hackathon at Github HQ, in San Francisco!

What is Horizons?

Horizons is a group of 7 Hack Club flagship hackathons taking place in all the continents(except Antarctica ofc), Arcana is the Horizons event in Singapore, Asia. <br />Read more about Horizons at horizons.hackclub.com!


Can I participate?

Yes, you can as long as you meet the requirements mentioned below:

1. You're 18 or younger and above 13 years of age
2. You qualify by shipping quality projects and don't engage in fraudulent activities in an attempt to cheat us.

Qualification- 

How to qualify?
In order to qualify for Arcana, you'll need to spend 35 hours working on one or multiple technical projects and ship them on the Horizons platform. Once they're reviewed and approved, you can buy a ticket to the event from there for free!

Can I participate?

Yes, you can as long as you meet the requirements mentioned below:

1. You're 18 or younger and above 13 years of age
2. You qualify by shipping quality projects and don't engage in fraudulent activities in an attempt to cheat us.


What kind of projects can I ship?
You can ship any type of technical projects- spanning from websites to hardware!
You have to use either hackatime or lapse to track your time. The process of shipping software projects is normally just submitting the github repo and a demo link, for hardware projects- check out the guide here!



Travel

Travelling to Arcana-
Arcana is in Singapore, a small island nation in South-East Asia. Singapore is pretty well connected to the rest of the world, below you can find some information which can help you plan your travel-
              
Travel Stipends
Our aim is to make this event accessible to everyone regardless of their financial or geographical background, so we will provide travel stipends to whoever works for them. You can get the travel stipends from Horizons shop at the rate of 8.5$/hr, which you can spend towards your flights, visa etc.
                  

Flying in and out of Singapore
Singapore has one main airport- Singapore Changi, it is one of the best airports in the world and is very well connected to the rest of the world. 
You should try to fly in before the evening of 31st July and fly out anytime after the evening of 2nd August.



Visa
Most nationalities can travel to Singapore visa-free for a certain number of days or can get a visa on arrival(VoA). If you require a visa to enter Singapore, we will try our best to help you out with it. More details surrounding the visa process will be announced as we move closer to the event.


Airport Pickups
Participants can request airport pickups, if requested- staff members will be there at the airport to pick them up and drop them to the venue. More details about this will be announced as we move closer to the event.
 


Parents Section-

We understand sending their kid to a different country brings in a lot of concerns for the parents and we totally respect them. Safety and well-being of the participants is our utmost priority and there will be no compromises with that.
Below listed are some resources which may help the parents clear their doubts regarding the event and the safety measures taken.


Parent's Guide
Closer to the event, we will release a detailed parent's guide which will include all the information regarding the event, safety and safeguarding measures, code of conduct, event waivers, emergency contact information and hotlines etc.
    

Parent's Call
If you have any doubts or concerns regarding the event, you can schedule a call and talk with the organisers of the event directly. Email us at arcana@horizons.hackclub.com to schedule a call!
     
Parents Accompanying the Participant
We understand that some parents might want to accompany their kid for travel and the event. While that's totally fine, Hack Club won't be able to cover any expenses for the parents, including flights, accommodation, or other travel costs. 
    
Parents won't be allowed inside the venue during the event, but they're welcome to briefly stop by for 5-10 minutes to check in and make sure everything feels comfortable.
